About Smart Health Navigator

Smart Health Navigator is our B.Tech 3rd year (6th semester) Computer Science & Engineering mini project, designed to bridge the gap between healthcare and technology using the power of Machine Learning.

Our Vision

We aim to make healthcare assistance smarter, faster, and more accessible for everyone. Our project is a step toward transforming symptom analysis through intelligent prediction models.

Who We Are

We are a team of enthusiastic undergraduate students from the Computer Science & Engineering department. With a shared interest in Artificial Intelligence and its real-world applications, we've created this project to demonstrate how technology can support better healthcare outcomes.

Project Overview

Smart Health Navigator is an AI-based web platform that allows users to input their symptoms and receive likely disease predictions. The system also provides detailed health insights including descriptions, precautions, medications, diet plans, and workout tips.

How It Works

Using a machine learning model trained on a rich dataset of symptoms and diagnoses, our application processes user inputs to identify possible illnesses. The goal is to assist users in making informed decisions before seeking medical consultation.

Why This Matters

In an age where digital health is evolving rapidly, we believe this tool can serve as a reliable, first-step assistant—especially in areas with limited access to immediate medical facilities.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with intelligent health insights and promote proactive well-being through technology. We want our project to reflect the real-world utility of the concepts we’ve learned in the classroom.

Thank You

We sincerely thank our faculty, mentors, and peers for guiding us throughout this journey.
We hope Smart Health Navigator can be a valuable contribution to tech-assisted healthcare innovation.
